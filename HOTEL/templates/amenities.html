In-room appliances

Our rooms at Ocean Vista are equipped with a range of high-end appliances to ensure your comfort and convenience. Each room features a personal safe to securely store your valuables, along with a fully stocked wet bar/mini bar offering a selection of premium beverages. Deluxe and suite guests enjoy spacious walk-in closets for added luxury and comfort. Unwind in a deep bathtub or enjoy the invigorating experience of a waterfall shower. Private Beach

Our private beach is a serene, sectioned man-made and custom landscaped cove is designed for the ultimate seaside experience. Snorkeling at Ocean Vista is an unforgettable experience, enhanced by our commitment to conservation. We also have our own state-protected coral reef, carefully nurtured to maintain its natural beauty and biodiversity. Guests can explore the vibrant underwater world, discovering colorful marine life amidst the thriving coral ecosystem as part of our ongoing conservation efforts in coop with the state marine biology community.

The Ocean Vista Private beach offers many of the same amenities as our pool, including beach equipment rentals, food and drink services, and poolside-style seating with umbrellas. A mini boardwalk adds to the charm, and the beach is regularly maintained by a dedicated team of janitors and cleaners, ensuring a pristine environment for all guests.

Hotel Transportation

Ocean Vista offers convenient shuttle services to a variety of popular locations, including LAX for a $60 fee (to and from). Deluxe members enjoy complimentary shuttle access, while standard and suite guests can purchase a $45 shuttle day pass or opt for the $120 package that includes unlimited shuttle use throughout their stay. Our shuttle can take you to beautiful LA beaches, downtown LA, amusement parks like Universal Studios and Disneyland, and more. We also offer special disability-accessible shuttles and can arrange private transportation, including family SUVs, luxury cars, or sports cars. Shuttle services are available from 7 AM to 11 PM daily.
